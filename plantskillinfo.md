# Plant Skill Information

## Version 1: Research Criteria
When the `plant` skill is used to add a new plant to the diary, it researches the following UK-specific (London-calibrated) details:

### 🌿 Plant Identity & Classification
*   **Names:** English name, Latin (botanical) name, and Family.
*   **Category:** Classification as a houseplant, flower, herb, fruit, or vegetable.
*   **Variety:** Specific cultivar details (e.g., 'Genovese' for Basil).

### 🗓️ UK Growing Schedule
*   **Sowing:** Precise weeks for sowing indoors and outdoors.
*   **Establishment:** Timing for hardening off and planting out.
*   **Harvesting:** Expected harvest window based on the UK climate.
*   **Frost Calibration:** Dates are adjusted for a Last Frost in Week 15 (mid-April) and First Frost in Week 44 (early November).

### 🛠️ Care & Characteristics
*   **Physical Traits:** Mature height, spread, and recommended container size.
*   **Requirements:** Specific sun exposure and watering/feeding needs.
*   **Maintenance:** Instructions for sowing, deadheading, and propagation.
*   **Seed Saving:** Guidance on how to save seeds for the next season.

### 🐛 Health & Ecology
*   **Companion Planting:** Which plants grow well alongside it.
*   **Common Problems:** Identifying typical pests or diseases.
*   **Fun Fact:** A unique bit of trivia about the plant.

### 📸 Visuals & Tracking
*   **Image Sourcing:** High-quality images of the plant, seed, flower, seedling, and fruit (if applicable).
*   **Growth Tracking:** For flowers, fruits and vegetables, it can initialize tracking for the "First Flower Date."
